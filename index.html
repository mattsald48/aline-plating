About Us

Airline Plating where all your custom plating needs are met. We started in 1979 and have been serving our customers non stop ever since. Specializing in decorative plating and polishing on anything from cars, rims, motorcycles, to door hardware. If you have a restoration project or just want some shine let us help you.

We can turn that old car or bike into show quality. Airline Plating has done it all from '57 Chevy's to '05 H2 Hummers and even old antiques. If chroming is not your thing we also provide other plating services and even polishing. So if you want to bling and shine bring it to Airline.

Made Custom Just For You

With over 40 years of experience in the below finishes and services we are convinced that you will walk away satisfied with your product. While other places only single dip your piece we triple dip to ensure you get a shine like no other.
